SaoPauloDojo

Welcome to São Paulo, Brazil!

The Dojo@São Paulo was founded in July/2007 and the meetings are held on Monday nights at the Institute of Mathematics and Statistics - University Of São Paulo (IME-USP). Join us at dojo_sp@groups.google.com (mailling list in Portuguese).
